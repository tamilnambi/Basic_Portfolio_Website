Personal Interests and Hobbies


Apart from my professional pursuits, I am passionate about traveling and photography. I find inspiration in exploring new places and cultures, meeting people from different backgrounds, and capturing the beauty of the world through my camera lens.

As a travel enthusiast, I have visited 9 states of India and 4 countries outside India, which have broadened my perspective and enriched my understanding of the world. I believe that traveling is not only a way to escape routine, but also a means to learn and grow as a person.

Photography is another hobby that I enjoy in my free time. I love capturing moments and emotions through my camera, whether it's a beautiful landscape, a candid portrait, or an abstract composition. I am self-taught and constantly learning new techniques and styles to improve my skills and express my creativity.
